Candy Stripper

View at full scale



Candy Stripper is a Japanese fashion brand. Their website is an example of simple web design that conveys the brand's aesthetic and concept.

In the site's header, there's a small, almost unnoticeable image of a panda. When you hover over the panda, a small menu of colors pops up. When you click one of these colors, the navigation items all change to that color. This adds a little playfulness to the website that isn't overwhelming or annoying. There's nothing to indicate that you should click on these colors; it's up to the user to explore and find out on their own.

Most of the site's navigation elements are just links, except for the "Online Store" and "Blog" buttons, which reveal drop down menus on hover.

The main feature of the front page is a slideshow. The image in the slide show changes opacity on hover. There's a small menu beneath it which indicates which slide is showing and allows users to click on a specific slide.
